Risk appetite is the risk needed to achieve strategic objectives.
Risk tolerance is how comfortable you feel about the risk.
Risk capacity is how much you can afford with the risk.

Risk Handling:
- Accept:  Identify and log, no action  
- Avoid:  Remove all possibility of loss, not realistic
- Control:  Analyze and learn risks, prepare alternative plans
- Exploit:  Maximize the chance the risk happens
- Mitigate:  Limit impact of risk
- Reduce:  Minimize the chance
- Share:  Risk Distribution: a self insurance method by spreading the burden of loss
- Transfer:  Transfer the risk to others (insurance policy)

Cyber Hygiene refers to steps that computer users can take to improve their cybersecurity and better protect themselves & others online.
